# Flow: Create a New Profile from Feed

**Performed by:** All personas
**Starts on:** Feed page
**Ends on:** Feed page (new profile card visible)

## Steps

1. On Feed, click "**+**" button under Your Portfolios/Projects panel
   OR click "**+ Add a Profile**" in the header Profile dropdown.
2. Navigates to Profile Builder.
3. Fill in Tab 1 fields.
4. Click **NEXT** to advance through tabs, or **SAVE** on the final tab.
5. After save, navigate back to Feed.
6. New profile card appears in the relevant panel.
7. New profile visible when clicking "Impact Profile" option.

## Notes

- Both **NEXT + SAVE** and direct **SAVE** (on final tab) workflows create the profile.
